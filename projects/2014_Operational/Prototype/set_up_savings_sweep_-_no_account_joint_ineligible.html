How it works

   •  If one or more transactions take your 360 Checking balance below $0 and you have enough money in your 360

      Savings to cover it, we’ll just transfer over the total amount needed at the end of the day.

   •  There are no charges or fees for using Savings Sweep.

   •  If a transaction exceeds the amount you have available in your 360 Savings, it’ll just be declined.

   •  The account ownership of your 360 Savings and 360 Checking must match. (e.g. If you have a joint acount holder        

       on your 360 Checking, that same person must be on the Savings Sweep account.)

 

Keep in mind, your 360 Savings has a limit of 6 withdrawals per month (it's a federal regulation). Learn More.
